______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
                                                          Brace Expansion  
                                                      ======================== 
                                                      For Read-Write Commands
                                                      ========================
__________________________________________________________________________________
1.Comma-Separated Expansion:

                    echo  {item1,item2,item3}
                    touch {item1,item2,item3}
                    touch file_{A,B,C}.txt
                    mkdir dir_{1,2,3}
                    rm -r dir_{A,B,C}

__________________________________________________________________________________
2.Sequential Range Expansion:

                    echo {1..5}
                    echo {a..e}

__________________________________________________________________________________
3.Custom Step Values in Range Expansion:

                    echo {1..10..2}
                    echo {a..z..3}

__________________________________________________________________________________
4.Combining Brace Expansion with Other Characters:

                    mkdir /path/to/dir_{A,B,C}/subdir_{1..3}

__________________________________________________________________________________
5.Using Nested Brace Expansion:

                    echo {A,B,C}_{1,2,3}
                    echo {a..c}{1..3}

__________________________________________________________________________________
6.Using Brace Expansion with Shell Variables:

                    prefix="backup"
                    echo ${prefix}_{1..3}.tar.gz
______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
